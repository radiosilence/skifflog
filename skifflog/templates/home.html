Keeping track of time is annoying.

And time flies when you're having fun at The Skiff!

So I've built a time logging system that allows you to select your plan, and will warn you when you are nearing going over your allotted time.

You can choose how to split your days up (twelve hours, six hours) and have your time rounded up to this depending on how you feel hours should be allotted.
